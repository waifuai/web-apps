Algorithm Explanation

This demo showcases an algorithm designed to prevent feature underutilization. By dynamically adjusting responses based on your interactions, the system ensures you progressively discover a wider range of capabilities, not just the most obvious ones.

Initially, the system responds with the most direct match to your input. However, as you repeat similar inputs, it intelligently shifts to suggest less frequent but equally valuable features, encouraging exploration and comprehensive understanding.
