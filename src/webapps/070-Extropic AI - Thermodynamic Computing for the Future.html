Revolutionary Approach to AI Hardware

Extropic AI is pioneering a paradigm shift in artificial intelligence hardware. We're developing a full-stack hardware platform that harnesses matter's natural fluctuations as a computational resource for Generative AI.

Our approach extends hardware scaling beyond the constraints of digital computing, enabling AI accelerators that are orders of magnitude faster and more energy-efficient than traditional digital processors.

The Power of Energy-Based Models (EBMs)

At the core of our technology are Energy-Based Models, which offer a unique solution to the challenges faced by traditional AI hardware. EBMs excel in low-data regimes and are particularly effective for modeling tail events in mission-critical applications.

Extropic Accelerators: Harnessing Randomness

Our accelerators implement EBMs directly as parameterized stochastic analog circuits. This approach achieves significant improvements in both runtime and energy efficiency for algorithms based on sampling from complex landscapes.

The operational principle is analogous to Brownian motion, where we create programmable randomness through carefully designed nano-fabricated circuits.

Superconducting and Semiconductor Solutions

Extropic is developing two main types of hardware:

  • Superconducting chips: Highly energy-efficient, operating at low temperatures, ideal for high-value customers like governments and banks.
  • Semiconductor devices: Room-temperature operation, suitable for widespread adoption, potentially bringing Extropic acceleration to every home.

Software Layer and Compilation

To support our diverse hardware, we're building a software layer that compiles abstract EBM specifications into hardware control language. This approach, based on factor graphs, allows our accelerators to handle programs too large for any single analog core.
