String Theory Basics: A Quantum Computational Perspective

Welcome to our exploration of String Theory from a quantum computational viewpoint. This page will introduce you to the fundamental concepts of String Theory and how they relate to quantum computing.

1. What is String Theory?

String Theory proposes that the fundamental constituents of the universe are not point-like particles, but tiny, vibrating strings of energy. These strings vibrate in different ways, giving rise to all the particles and forces we observe.
